About Me

What do I do?

Some passions of mine include art, dance and music, which have all been part of my life for at least 7 years.

❮ ❯

  • Art

    With drawing, I have been doing so since I can remember, but started lessons at age 7 as well. I mainly draw with pencils/colored pencils, but I enjoy being involved in any type of drawing and illustration. Although I sometimes spend hours on one piece, seeing the final product makes it worth it.

  • Music

    I have been playing piano since I was about 7, and I still enjoy doing so every day. I enjoy being able to play advanced pieces that I know are because I worked hard to play it. For me, playing piano is a skill that relaxes me. I also play guitar as a hobby and have been playing flute since 4th grade.

  • Dance

    Similarly, I have been dancing for almost 10 years, and have been competing since 7th grade. Dance has always been something I love doing. Being able to see myself constantly improve and do things I couldn't do before fuels and motivates me to work harder and improver further.
